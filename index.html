Welcome


I am a Research Scientist in deep learning at Salesforce Research (MetaMind). My current research interests include machine learning on multimodal data, combining natural language with computer vision, zero-shot learning, inclusion of prior knowledge and expert knowledge, and deep learning for specialized domains such as medicine and engineering. I also conducted research at the intersection of applied mathematics and energy. I studied statistics, optimization, and machine learning to improve energy generation and efficiency.


I earned my PhD and MS from the University of California, Berkeley in Mechanical Engineering. I completed my BASci in Engineering Science from the University of Toronto.
